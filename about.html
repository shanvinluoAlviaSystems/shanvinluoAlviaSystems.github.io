About Us

Welcome to Dbayeh

RTV is based in Dbayeh, a coastal town in Lebanon, famed for its "charming" blend of never-ending construction and the delightful sound of traffic. It's a must-visit for those who adore navigating through crowded shopping malls and enjoying the "exquisite" experience of packed beach resorts.

Impact Through Information

At RTV, truth comes before profit. We are committed to providing our audience with news that they can trust, no matter the situation. Our dedication to factual accuracy and unbiased reporting sets us apart, ensuring that we deliver the real story, every time, regardless of financial offers or external pressures. This makes us a standout choice for discerning viewers who value integrity in journalism.

Fighting Poverty in Lebanon

Our mission is clear and ambitious – to utilize the profits from RTV to combat and ultimately cure poverty in Lebanon. We believe in the power of media to not just report on the world but to change it. By allocating our financial resources towards development projects and direct aid, we are working to create a sustainable impact that not only alleviates immediate needs but also fosters long-term growth and prosperity in our communities.

What We Seek

Our vision at RTV is to transform how news is perceived and used. By reporting on pressing local and global issues, we aim to not only inform but also inspire our audience to take action. The revenue generated from our operations is directed towards funding charitable causes, particularly those featured in our stories. This approach turns each piece of news into a step towards societal improvement, making every story count towards a greater good.

Credibility

Non Biased News Channel

At RTV, we are honored to proclaim our status as the foremost impartial news channel in the Middle East—a testament to our enduring ethos of balanced reporting. This commitment is mirrored in Lebanon's unique heritage, a nation united under a singular religious and political identity spanning from 1861 to 2024, reflecting our shared vision of harmony and solidarity.

Imopact Of the Week

GreenLeb Movement

Thanks to the environmental commitment of Lebanese civilians, the GreenLeb Movement has raised over $700,000 to combat pollution across Lebanon. These funds have enabled us to significantly reduce pollution by more than 60% in key areas, focusing on rivers, seas, and land. Through cleaning operations and educational initiatives, we are not only revitalizing Lebanon’s natural landscapes but also empowering communities to sustain these efforts for future generations. Join us as we continue to restore and preserve the beauty of our environment.
